About the KnowMed System

The Personal Medicine Inventory System is designed to assist patients in managing their medication schedules effectively, ensuring they never miss a dose. Unlike traditional inventory systems that focus on stock management, our solution emphasizes timely dose reminders and easy access to comprehensive medicine information.

With the increasing complexity of medication schedules, especially for chronic illnesses or multiple prescriptions, many patients struggle to maintain adherence. This can lead to missed doses, reduced medication effectiveness, and even health complications. Our system addresses this challenge by providing accurate and customizable alarms to remind patients to take their medication as prescribed.
